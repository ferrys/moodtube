As an authenticated user, I can click on a GIF/image and it will be enlarged in a pop-up. I am then given the option to “Like,” “Dislike,” “Tweet,” or “Copy Link” for the GIF/image displayed below it. 
	
If I click on “Like” and I have not liked the GIF/image in the past, it will be saved to my Likes. If I have liked the GIF/image in the past, it will be removed from my Likes. If I click on “Dislike” and I have not disliked the GIF/image in the past it will be saved to my Dislikes. If I have disliked the GIF/image in the past, it will be removed from my Dislikes. 

If I click on “Tweet,” I will be shown a pop-up where I can draft a Twitter post. I can choose to write a Tweet and share. 

If I click on “Copy Link,” a hyperlink will be copied to the clipboard.
